About

This project was inspired by a YouTube documentary titled "The Fall of the Simpsons". In particular, a figure which trends the average IMDB user rating of each Simpsons espisode over time. We started to wonder what else could be done with IMDB data. The answer is a lot! Too much in fact. To limit scope, we selected 26 TV series similar to the Simpsons and built a dashboard around the idea of the figures below. Users can add up to seven series to the trend and look for patterns and interactions.

The project uses two sources of data. First, the .tsv files which IMDB releases nightly. These contain a subset of the actual IMDB database. Second, the IMDbPy package was used for information not not available in the .tsv files. The site is powered by an Amazon RDS MySQL instance and heroku running a python Flask application on the back end. On the front end, we used plotly.js to render most of the figures.



Many fans consider the turning point for the series to be an episode titled "The Principal and the Pauper" from season 8. Actor Harry Shearer has referred to it as "a horrible mistake". Episodes more recent than season 8 are often referred to as "Zombie Simpsons", implying the show has become a lifeless shell of its former self.
